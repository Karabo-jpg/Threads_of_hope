## Getting Started

### Creating an Account

1. Visit the Threads of Hope website
2. Click **"Sign Up"**
3. Fill in your personal information
4. Select your role (Woman, Donor, or NGO Partner)
5. Verify your email address
6. Wait for account approval (NGO Partners only)

### Logging In

1. Go to the login page
2. Enter your email and password
3. Click **"Login"**
4. You'll be directed to your personalized dashboard

### Language Selection

Threads of Hope supports multiple languages:
- **English** (EN)
- **Swahili** (SW)
- **French** (FR)

Change language using the dropdown in the top right corner.

## For Women

### Finding Training Programs

1. Click **"Training"** in the navigation menu
2. Browse available programs by category:
   - Sewing & Tailoring
   - Cooking & Baking
   - Hairdressing & Beauty
   - Computer Skills
   - Business Management
   - Handicrafts
   - And more!

### Enrolling in a Program

1. Click on a program to view details
2. Review the program description, duration, and requirements
3. Click **"Enroll Now"**
4. Wait for approval from the program organizer
5. Once approved, you can start learning!

### Tracking Your Progress

1. Go to **"My Enrollments"**
2. View your active programs
3. Update your progress regularly
4. Upload proof of progress (photos, projects)
5. Complete assessments as required

### Earning Certificates

1. Complete 100% of the program curriculum
2. Maintain good attendance (if applicable)
3. Pass required assessments
4. Certificate will be automatically issued
5. Download your certificate from your dashboard
6. Share your achievement!

### Viewing Your Badges

1. Navigate to your profile
2. Click on **"My Badges"**
3. View all earned badges and achievements
4. Share on social media (optional)

## For Donors

### Making a Donation

1. Click **"Make Donation"** on your dashboard
2. Choose donation type:
   - **Specific Child**: Support a particular child's needs
   - **Woman**: Support women's empowerment programs
   - **NGO**: General support for an organization
   - **Training Program**: Fund specific training initiatives
   - **General**: Unrestricted funds
3. Enter donation amount
4. Select payment method
5. Add personal message (optional)
6. Choose to remain anonymous or not
7. Complete payment
8. Receive instant receipt via email

### Tracking Your Impact

1. Go to **"My Donations"**
2. View all past donations
3. Click on a donation to see detailed impact reports
4. See how funds have been allocated
5. View photos and updates from beneficiaries
6. Read impact metrics and outcomes

### Viewing Beneficiaries

1. Navigate to **"Children"** or **"Programs"**
2. See profiles of children or programs you've supported
3. View updates and progress
4. Send messages to NGO partners

### Setting Up Recurring Donations

1. When making a donation, check **"Make this recurring"**
2. Select frequency (monthly, quarterly, annually)
3. Manage recurring donations in your profile settings
4. Cancel or modify anytime

## For NGO Partners

### Registering a Child

1. Click **"Register Child"** on your dashboard
2. Fill in required information:
   - Personal details (name, DOB, gender)
   - Current status (orphan, vulnerable, rescued, etc.)
   - Medical history
   - Education level
   - Family background
3. Upload supporting documents
4. Submit for review
5. Child profile is created with unique case number

### Managing Child Welfare Records

1. Go to **"Children"**
2. Click on a child's profile
3. View complete history and timeline
4. Add new events:
   - Health checkups
   - Medical treatments
   - Education updates
   - Placement changes
   - Counseling sessions
5. Upload photos and documents
6. Track all interventions

### Creating Training Programs

1. Navigate to **"Training"** > **"Create Program"**
2. Enter program details:
   - Title and description
   - Category and skill level
   - Duration and schedule
   - Maximum participants
   - Location (physical or online)
   - Curriculum outline
3. Set enrollment criteria
4. Publish program
5. Manage enrollments and approve applicants

### Managing Enrollments

1. Go to **"Training"** > **"Manage Programs"**
2. Select a program
3. View enrollment requests
4. Approve or reject applications
5. Track participant progress
6. Issue certificates upon completion

### Creating Impact Reports

1. Navigate to **"Donations"**
2. Select a donation you've received
3. Click **"Create Impact Report"**
4. Enter information:
   - Amount used
   - Category (education, healthcare, food, etc.)
   - Description of activities
   - Number of beneficiaries
   - Outcomes and metrics
5. Upload photos and receipts
6. Submit report to donor

### Collaboration Requests

1. Click **"Collaboration"** in the menu
2. Browse available collaboration opportunities
3. Or create your own request:
   - Title and description
   - Type (joint program, resource sharing, funding, etc.)
   - Duration and location
   - Required skills/resources
4. Respond to other organizations' requests
5. Communicate via built-in messaging

### Sharing Resources

1. Go to **"Collaboration"** > **"Resources"**
2. Click **"Share Resource"**
3. Upload document or link
4. Add title and description
5. Set access level:
   - Public
   - Verified users only
   - NGOs only
   - Private
6. Publish resource

## For Administrators

### Approving Users

1. Navigate to **"Admin Panel"** > **"Users"**
2. Filter by **"Pending Approval"**
3. Review user application details
4. Check NGO registration documents (if applicable)
5. Click **"Approve"** or **"Reject"**
6. User receives notification

### Viewing System Statistics

1. Go to **"Admin Dashboard"**
2. View key metrics:
   - Total users by role
   - Children registered
   - Donations received
   - Active training programs
   - Completion rates
3. Filter by date range
4. Export reports

### Managing Audit Logs

1. Navigate to **"Audit Logs"**
2. View all system activities
3. Filter by:
   - User
   - Action type
   - Date range
   - Entity type
4. Export logs for compliance

### System Health Monitoring

1. Go to **"System Health"**
2. Check:
   - Database status
   - Server uptime
   - Memory usage
   - Error rates
3. Receive alerts for issues

## Common Features

### Messages

#### Sending a Message

1. Click **"Messages"** > **"Compose"**
2. Select recipient
3. Enter subject and message
4. Attach files (optional)
5. Send

#### Reading Messages

1. Go to **"Messages"** > **"Inbox"**
2. Click on a message to read
3. Reply or archive

### Notifications

1. Click the bell icon in the header
2. View all notifications
3. Click to see details
4. Mark as read
5. Manage notification preferences in settings

### Profile Management

1. Click on your avatar > **"Profile"**
2. Update personal information
3. Change profile picture
4. Update contact details
5. Set notification preferences
6. Change password

### Changing Password

1. Go to **"Settings"** > **"Security"**
2. Click **"Change Password"**
3. Enter current password
4. Enter new password
5. Confirm new password
6. Save changes

### Enabling 2FA (Two-Factor Authentication)

1. Navigate to **"Settings"** > **"Security"**
2. Click **"Enable 2FA"**
3. Scan QR code with authenticator app
4. Enter verification code
5. Save backup codes

## FAQ

### General Questions

**Q: Is Threads of Hope free to use?**  
A: Yes, the platform is free for all users. Training programs may have associated costs determined by the organizing NGO.

**Q: How is my data protected?**  
A: We use industry-standard encryption, secure servers, and comply with data protection regulations. See our Privacy Policy for details.

**Q: Can I use Threads of Hope on my mobile phone?**  
A: Yes! The web application is mobile-responsive. A native mobile app is also available.

### For Women

**Q: How long do training programs take?**  
A: Program duration varies from a few weeks to several months. Check individual program details.

**Q: Do I need to pay for training?**  
A: Some programs are free, while others may have a small fee. This is clearly indicated on each program.

**Q: Can I enroll in multiple programs?**  
A: Yes, you can enroll in multiple programs simultaneously.

**Q: What if I can't complete a program?**  
A: You can drop out anytime, but we encourage you to communicate with the program organizer.

### For Donors

**Q: How do I know my donation is being used properly?**  
A: NGOs are required to submit detailed impact reports showing exactly how funds were used, with photos and receipts.

**Q: Can I donate to a specific child?**  
A: Yes, you can browse child profiles and donate directly to their needs.

**Q: Is my donation tax-deductible?**  
A: Depending on your country and the receiving organization, it may be. Check with your tax advisor.

**Q: Can I remain anonymous?**  
A: Yes, you can choose to make anonymous donations.

### For NGO Partners

**Q: How do I get approved as an NGO?**  
A: Submit your organization's registration documents during sign-up. Admin will review and approve within 48 hours.

**Q: Can I import existing child records?**  
A: Yes, contact support for bulk import assistance.

**Q: How do I request collaboration with another NGO?**  
A: Use the Collaboration feature to create requests or respond to existing ones.
